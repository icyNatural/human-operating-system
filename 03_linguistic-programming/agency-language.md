# Agency Language

## Core Idea
Some forms of language increase perceived authorship and action-readiness.

## Why It Matters
Agency language can change how the mind prepares behavior.

## Mechanism
Phrases built around choosing, directing, allowing, and committing alter interpretation and control signals.

## Operational Steps
1. Catch passive inner speech.
2. Replace it with agentive wording.
3. Keep the wording specific.
4. Test whether behavior changes.

## Real-World Applications
- Discipline
- Recovery
- Focus
- Decision making

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Record your strongest agency phrases.
