# Identity Language Design

## Core Idea
Identity is reinforced by repeated self-description.

## Why It Matters
Words can stabilize weak identities or open room for growth identities.

## Mechanism
Self-referential language updates narrative expectations and behavioral consistency.

## Operational Steps
1. Remove rigid defeat language.
2. Use transitional identity language.
3. Anchor identity in behavior.
4. Repeat across time.

## Real-World Applications
- Reinvention
- Recovery
- Performance growth
- Consistency building

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Track phrases that feel false versus phrases that feel stretchable but believable.
