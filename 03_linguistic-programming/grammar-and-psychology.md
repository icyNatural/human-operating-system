# Grammar and Psychology

## Core Idea
Sentence structure influences how experience is framed.

## Why It Matters
Grammar affects agency, time horizon, emotional tone, and responsibility.

## Mechanism
Passive versus active wording, fixed versus evolving identity statements, and urgency pacing alter internal processing.

## Operational Steps
1. Compare two phrasings of the same state.
2. Observe the felt difference.
3. Choose the structure that supports function.
4. Repeat until it becomes natural.

## Real-World Applications
- Self-talk
- Journaling
- Coaching
- Performance language

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Collect sentence pairs that produce different states.
