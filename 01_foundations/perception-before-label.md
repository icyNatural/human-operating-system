# Perception Before Label

## Core Idea
There is a difference between raw perception and immediate conceptual labeling.

## Why It Matters
Quick labels compress reality and can trap perception in old patterns.

## Mechanism
Sensory data arrives, then language and memory rapidly classify it.

## Operational Steps
1. Pause before naming.
2. Notice texture, motion, tone, and pattern.
3. Delay judgment briefly.
4. Compare raw perception with your label.

## Real-World Applications
- Better observation
- Less projection
- Stronger awareness
- Sharper pattern recognition
