# Attention Mechanics

## Core Idea
Attention is not passive noticing. It is selective energetic allocation.

## Why It Matters
What attention rests on becomes psychologically amplified.

## Mechanism
Attention filters salience, strengthens certain signals, and shapes interpretation and behavior.

## Operational Steps
1. Identify where attention is resting.
2. Ask whether it is useful.
3. Reassign attention intentionally.
4. Repeat until redirection becomes easier.

## Real-World Applications
- Focus
- Emotional control
- Perception training
- Reduced reactivity

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Track repeated attention traps and successful redirects.
