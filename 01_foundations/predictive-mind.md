# Predictive Mind

## Core Idea
The mind does not simply react to reality. It continuously predicts incoming experience and updates itself through feedback.

## Why It Matters
Prediction affects emotion, attention, action, and perceived control.

## Mechanism
The brain builds models, compares expectation against incoming data, and adjusts behavior or interpretation.

## Operational Steps
1. Notice your default expectation.
2. Observe what state that expectation creates.
3. Replace weak prediction language with deliberate framing.
4. Track how behavior changes.

## Real-World Applications
- Stress regulation
- Study performance
- Social confidence
- Recovery from drift
