# Identity Plasticity

## Core Idea
Identity is partly stable pattern and partly trainable construction.

## Why It Matters
People act from who they believe they are. Changing identity changes probability of action.

## Mechanism
Repeated behavior, internal narration, and social reinforcement update self-modeling.

## Operational Steps
1. Define the identity pattern you want.
2. Specify behaviors that prove it.
3. Repeat those behaviors.
4. Record evidence instead of waiting for a feeling.

## Real-World Applications
- Discipline
- Confidence
- Personal reinvention
- Long-term growth

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Document moments where behavior shifted your self-concept.
