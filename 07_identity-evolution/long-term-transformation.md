# Long-Term Transformation

## Core Idea
Deep change happens through repeated adjustment, not one-time intensity.

## Why It Matters
This protects against fantasy, impatience, and collapse cycles.

## Mechanism
Small repeated actions reshape identity, state tolerance, and self-organization.

## Operational Steps
1. Choose a long arc.
2. Reduce goals to repeatable behaviors.
3. Track stability more than hype.
4. Keep refining.

## Real-World Applications
- Physical transformation
- Mental change
- Habit installation
- Character building
