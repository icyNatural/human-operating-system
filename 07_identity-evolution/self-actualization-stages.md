# Self-Actualization Stages

## Core Idea
Human development can be seen as progressive self-organization rather than static personality.

## Why It Matters
Stages help orient growth without pretending development is random.

## Mechanism
Each stage increases awareness, integration, responsibility, and conscious authorship.

## Operational Steps
1. Identify the current growth edge.
2. Describe the next more coherent version of self.
3. Build behaviors that embody it.
4. Review evidence over time.

## Real-World Applications
- Personal growth
- Career evolution
- Character development
- Self-authorship

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Write stage markers from your own life.
