# Behavioral Proof of Identity

## Core Idea
Identity becomes believable when supported by repeated action.

## Why It Matters
Without proof, identity language stays fantasy.

## Mechanism
Behavioral evidence updates self-trust and prediction.

## Operational Steps
1. Define the identity.
2. List proving behaviors.
3. Repeat them consistently.
4. Review the evidence.

## Real-World Applications
- Discipline
- Confidence
- Reinvention
- Skill growth

## Observations / Experimental Notes
