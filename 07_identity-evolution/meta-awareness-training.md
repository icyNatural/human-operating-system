# Meta-Awareness Training

## Core Idea
Meta-awareness is the ability to notice mental process while it is happening.

## Why It Matters
Without it, you can only react after the fact.

## Mechanism
Attention turns toward thought, emotion, and impulse in real time.

## Operational Steps
1. Pause during active experience.
2. Name the process, not just the content.
3. Distinguish thought from observer.
4. Redirect if needed.

## Real-World Applications
- Emotion regulation
- Impulse control
- Better decisions
- Conscious growth
