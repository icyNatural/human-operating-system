# Feedback Loops

## Core Idea
Behavior is maintained or changed through loops of action and consequence.

## Why It Matters
If you can see the loop, you can redesign it.

## Mechanism
Cue, interpretation, action, result, and reinforcement form recurring cycles.

## Operational Steps
1. Identify the recurring pattern.
2. Map trigger and reward.
3. Change one leverage point.
4. Re-test the loop.

## Real-World Applications
- Habits
- Emotional patterns
- Work consistency
- Self-correction

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Map one strong loop per week.
