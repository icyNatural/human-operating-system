# Decision Architecture

## Core Idea
Decisions improve when the process around them is designed instead of improvised.

## Why It Matters
Good structure reduces noise and impulsive distortion.

## Mechanism
Criteria, timing, emotional state, and framing shape outcomes.

## Operational Steps
1. Define the decision.
2. Define criteria.
3. Check state before choosing.
4. Decide inside a clear structure.

## Real-World Applications
- Career moves
- Daily planning
- Social decisions
- Personal strategy

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Note where bad state produced weak decisions.
