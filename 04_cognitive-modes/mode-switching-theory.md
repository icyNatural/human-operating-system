# Mode Switching Theory

## Core Idea
The mind can operate through different repeatable modes, each with its own strengths and blind spots.

## Why It Matters
Intentional switching improves adaptability.

## Mechanism
Different modes change question style, emotional tone, perception bias, and action pattern.

## Operational Steps
1. Name the current mode.
2. Define the desired mode.
3. Shift posture, language, and question style.
4. Re-enter the task.

## Real-World Applications
- Writing
- Debate
- Reflection
- Strategy

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Document which cues help you switch fastest.
