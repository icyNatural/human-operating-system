# Silent Perception Mode

## Core Idea
Silent perception mode emphasizes observation before explanation.

## Why It Matters
It reduces projection and increases contact with raw pattern.

## Mechanism
By delaying interpretation, perception broadens.

## Operational Steps
1. Be still.
2. Observe without naming.
3. Notice pattern, motion, texture, relation.
4. Interpret only afterward.

## Real-World Applications
- Art
- Awareness practice
- Social reading
- Sensory grounding
