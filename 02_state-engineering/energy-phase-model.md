# Energy Phase Model

## Core Idea
Human performance shifts across the day in phases, not flat consistency.

## Why It Matters
Working with phase improves timing, focus, and recovery.

## Mechanism
Sleep, wake duration, activity load, and physiological state influence energy expression.

## Operational Steps
1. Identify current phase.
2. Match task intensity to phase.
3. Use recovery before collapse.
4. Review patterns over time.

## Real-World Applications
- Scheduling
- Work output
- Exercise timing
- Recovery planning

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Track your strongest and weakest daily windows.
