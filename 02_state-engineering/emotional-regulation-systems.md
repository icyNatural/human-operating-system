# Emotional Regulation Systems

## Core Idea
Emotion can be worked with as a regulatory pattern rather than only as a feeling.

## Why It Matters
Regulation changes performance, decision quality, and social behavior.

## Mechanism
Breathing, posture, language, interpretation, and attention all affect emotional state.

## Operational Steps
1. Identify the active state.
2. Locate the trigger.
3. Change body pattern.
4. Change language pattern.
5. Reassess after action.

## Real-World Applications
- Stress
- Conflict
- Recovery
- Focus transitions

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Document which interventions create the fastest shift.
