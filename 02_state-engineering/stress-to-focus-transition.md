# Stress to Focus Transition

## Core Idea
Stress energy can sometimes be redirected into useful focus instead of wasted agitation.

## Why It Matters
This increases function under pressure.

## Mechanism
Interpretation, breathing, posture, and task clarity can convert threat-like activation into directed effort.

## Operational Steps
1. Name the state accurately.
2. Reduce excess bodily escalation.
3. Shrink the task target.
4. Reframe pressure as directed challenge.
5. Begin immediately.

## Real-World Applications
- Deadlines
- Difficult conversations
- Performance pressure
- Recovery from overwhelm

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Track when stress became productive versus when it scattered.
