# Greek Mind Model

## Core Idea
Ancient Greek thought described the human being through multiple faculties rather than a single flat mind.

## Why It Matters
This model offers richer vocabulary for inner process.

## Mechanism
Faculties like nous, logos, thumos, and psyche describe distinct functions of perception, reason, drive, and life-force.

## Operational Steps
1. Learn the core faculties.
2. Observe which is dominant in a situation.
3. Use the model to interpret inner conflict.
4. Compare with modern terms.

## Real-World Applications
- Self-understanding
- Philosophy
- Regulation
- Character analysis

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Record situations where ancient vocabulary clarifies modern confusion.
