# Taoist Flow Control

## Core Idea
Taoist thought emphasizes effective alignment over forced strain.

## Why It Matters
It reveals when effort is intelligent versus when effort is friction.

## Mechanism
Reduced excess resistance creates more efficient action.

## Operational Steps
1. Notice where force is unnecessary.
2. Reduce strain.
3. Work with timing and pattern.
4. Act with less internal conflict.

## Real-World Applications
- Conversation
- Work rhythm
- Recovery
- Adaptation

## Observations / Experimental Notes
Track where reducing force improved outcome.
