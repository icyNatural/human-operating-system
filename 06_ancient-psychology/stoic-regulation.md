# Stoic Regulation

## Core Idea
Stoicism trains discernment between what can be governed and what cannot.

## Why It Matters
This reduces wasted disturbance and stabilizes action.

## Mechanism
Cognitive framing, voluntary discipline, and value anchoring reduce reactive suffering.

## Operational Steps
1. Separate controllable from uncontrollable.
2. Govern what is yours.
3. Release what is not.
4. Continue action without complaint.

## Real-World Applications
- Stress
- Social conflict
- Discipline
- Resilience
